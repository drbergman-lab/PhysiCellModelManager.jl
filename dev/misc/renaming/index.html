Renaming

Julia packages are supposed to follow certain conventions to be admitted to the General registry. In particular, it must end with .jl, be CamelCase, avoid jargon/acronyms (looking at you pcvct), and be descriptive. We want to clearly tie it to PhysiCell but not make it sound like a replacement for PhysiCell, i.e. not PhysiCell.jl. Here are the options brainstormed thus far:

  • PhysiCellVT.jl
  • PhysiVT.jl (possible confusion with the OpenVT project where VT = virtual tissue)
  • PhysiCellCohorts.jl
  • PhysiCellTrials.jl
  • PhysiVirtualTrials.jl
  • PhysiCellBatch.jl
  • PhysiBatch.jl
  • PhysiCellDB.jl
  • PhysiDB.jl (the clear name for make the database portion a separate package)
  • PhysiCell.jl (kinda self-important to assume this will be all the PhysiCell stuff in Julia)
